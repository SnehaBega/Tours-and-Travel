    Destination: Kathmandu

Highlights of Kathmandu:

  • Visit UNESCO World Heritage Sites
  • Explore the vibrant streets of Thamel
  • Witness the ancient architecture of Patan and Bhaktapur

Kathmandu, the capital city of Nepal, is a perfect blend of traditional and modern culture. From ancient temples and palaces to modern cafes and bars, the city has something to offer for everyone. With affordable accommodations and food, Kathmandu is a great destination for budget travelers.

Itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu and check-in at hotel
  • Day 2: Visit UNESCO World Heritage Sites in Kathmandu Valley
  • Day 3: Visit Patan and Bhaktapur, two ancient cities near Kathmandu
  • Day 4: Departure from Kathmandu

Cost Details:

The cost of the trip starts from Rs. 20,000 per person, which includes accommodation, transportation, and sightseeing. Airfare and meals are not included in the cost.

Essential Information:

  • Visa is required for most nationalities to enter Nepal
  • Spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November) are the best seasons to visit Kathmandu
  • Carry enough cash as most places don't accept credit/debit cards
